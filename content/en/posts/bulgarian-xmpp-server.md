Chatrix.One is a Bulgarian XMPP server for free, end-to-end encrypted communication, with no telemetry and no ads.

## Features

- Instant messaging with reactions and emoji support
- Image and file sharing of any type
- Location sharing
- Audio and video calls
- Voice messages
- Group chat
- Sent, delivered and read indicators
- Contact avatars
- Desktop sync
- Compatible with all major operating systems

## Why Chatrix.One?

- Built on the proven [XMPP](https://xmpp.org/about/technology-overview/) standard
- End-to-end encryption with OMEMO or OpenPGP
- Encrypted audio and video calls (DTLS-SRTP)
- RESPECTS and VALUES your privacy
- Does NOT require personal data to register
- Does NOT serve ads, banners or offers
- Does NOT require consent to unreasonable policies
- Runs on Ejabberd - an [open source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open-source_software) platform
- 100% compliance with available XEPs[^1]
- Decentralised architecture
- Server located in Switzerland, accessible worldwide
- Powered 100% by renewable energy!

Instructions on how to use the service, FAQ, rules and privacy policy are available at the official documentation page: [docs.chatrix.one](https://docs.chatrix.one)

[^1]: XMPP Extension Protocols. Compliance can be checked at any time at [compliance.conversations.im](https://compliance.conversations.im/server/chatrix.one/)
