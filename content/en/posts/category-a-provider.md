On 17.07.2024 Chatrix.One was included in the [XMPP Providers](https://providers.xmpp.net/) list! Even better, the service received the maximum score — **Category A** 🎉. The new badge reflecting this achievement is now available on the [status page](https://docs.chatrix.one/status/).

[![Chatrix.One Badge](https://data.xmpp.net/providers/v2/badges/chatrix.one.svg)](https://providers.xmpp.net/provider/chatrix.one/)

At the time of listing, the directory included 61 providers in total, of which only 16 are **Category A**.
